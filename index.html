Everything in one engagement

A complete strategic finance toolkit for small business owners — from monthly reporting to long-range planning.

Monthly Reporting

P&L review, KPI tracking, and key metric analysis — delivered monthly so you always know where the business stands.

  • Monthly Reporting Package: P&L Review with commentary
  • KPI dashboard tailored to your key business drivers
  • Written narrative explaining what changed and why

So you always know where the business stands — and why.

Financial Modeling

Scenario analysis, compensation modeling, and driver-based forecasts built for real decisions.

  • 3-scenario revenue model (base, upside, downside)
  • Headcount and compensation planning tied to growth milestones
  • Pricing sensitivity and margin analysis

Models built for your decisions, not retrofitted from a template.

Strategic Planning

Annual and long-range planning that aligns your financial targets with business strategy.

  • Annual operating plan with monthly targets
  • 3–5 year long-range financial plan
  • One-page strategic dashboard for leadership alignment

Your numbers and your strategy, finally pointing the same direction.

The Approach

Clarity, not complexity

We cut through the noise and build financial clarity from the ground up — translating complex data into decisions your entire team can act on.

Deep-dive financial diagnosis
We start with your numbers, not assumptions. Every engagement begins with a full financial review.
Custom models, not templates
Every model is built for your business — your drivers, your industry, your decisions.
Actionable recommendations
Deliverables that drive decisions — not reports that sit in a drawer.
Monthly reporting package
Tailored to your business — P&L review, KPI dashboard, cash flow summary, and key variance analysis delivered on a consistent cadence.
Monthly owner review call
A dedicated monthly session to walk through results, explain variances and drivers, and align on what the numbers mean for your decisions ahead.
Rolling forecast & budget management
Your budget stays alive — updated as the business evolves, so you're always planning against reality, not a stale spreadsheet.
Compensation scenario analysis
Model the bottom-line impact of adjusting bonus structures, commission plans, or salary increases before making the call.
Headcount & hiring analysis
Evaluate the true cost of a new hire — loaded comp, ramp time, revenue contribution — so you know exactly when it pencils out.
Marketing & campaign ROI
Before committing budget to a campaign, build the financial case — projected return, break-even volume, and downside scenarios.
